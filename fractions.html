🍕 Understanding Fractions

A simple guide for everyday cooking and baking

📖 Lesson 1: What Is a Fraction?

A fraction is a way to talk about a part of something.

Imagine you have a pizza. If you cut it into 4 equal pieces and you take 1 piece — you have one quarter of the pizza.

🍕 One slice = 1/4 of the pizza

We use fractions every day in the kitchen — for measuring cups, cutting food, and following recipes!

📖 Lesson 2: How to Read a Fraction

Every fraction has two numbers with a line between them.

1 4
One quarter
→

Top number = how many pieces you have

Bottom number = how many pieces the whole is cut into

1/2

One half — 1 piece out of 2

3/4

Three quarters — 3 pieces out of 4

📖 Lesson 4: Which Fraction Is Bigger?

This is easy to remember: a bigger bottom number means smaller pieces.

🧠 Tip: Think of it this way — if you cut a cake into 2 pieces, each piece is big. If you cut the same cake into 8 pieces, each piece is tiny.
